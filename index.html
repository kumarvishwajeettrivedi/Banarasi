बनारसीथंडाई

The Royal Elixir of Varanasi

Thandai is a traditional Indian drink, especially cherished during Holi and Maha Shivaratri. Originating from the vibrant streets of Banaras, it blends milk, nuts, and fragrant spices into a refreshing masterpiece. Deeply rooted in Ayurveda, thandai is believed to cool the body, aid digestion, and boost immunity. Its presence is found not only in homes but also in the hospital tradition of serving nourishing beverages to patients. Today, thandai is more than just a drink—it is a sip of history, culture, and festive joy that unites people across generations.
